The Game

Hybrid is a cooperative multiplayer game for two players so if you want to play, grab a friend. It’s an asymmetric stealth game, where one player is the Thief and the other is the Hacker. The thief moves around the map and is the eyes and ears on the ground. The hacker has access to the cameras and knows how to open up anything. Together you have to avoid the guards and complete the mission.
